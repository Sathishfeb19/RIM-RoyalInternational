STATE PROPHECIES

STATE: KERALA, INDIA

  • PROPHECY: JITHU CS was informed by the Holy Spirit that a great disaster will happen in Kerala which will be a sign for the coming revival in Kerala according to 1 Kings 19:12.
  • DATE OF THE PROPHECY: Unknown (Could be in April 2018)
  • DATE OF THE EVENT: 8 August 2018 to 20 August 2020
  • DATE OF FULFILLMENT: 8 August 2018
  • NEWS: Over 483 people died, and about one million people were evacuated, all the 14 districts of Kerala affected by the flood making it the worst more than a 100 years.
  • INVOLVEMENT: JITHU CS was given the opportunity to lead the prayer sessions during the Kerala flood 2018 in a seminary.
  • DECLARING THE DECREE: God through Jesus Christ by the power of the Holy Spirit confirmed the coming of a revival in Kerala to exalt His name because "salvation is found in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given to mankind by which we must be saved." Amen.

STATE: KERALA, INDIA

  • DREAM: JITHU CS thought of seeking the Lord in 2019 to know if there would be another flood in Kerala because of the Kerala flood 2018, and then very soon a flood hit Assam, later Kerala was also hit by a flood, killed 121 lives, affected 13 districts and 1038 villages, causing significant damage to 21,264 houses. The next year JITHU CS saw a dream of flood in Kerala affecting Ernakulam and Trivandrum.
  • DATE OF THE DREAM: 20 June 2020
  • DATE OF THE EVENT: 7 August 2020
  • DATE OF FULFILLMENT: 7 August 2020
  • NEWS: During the heavy rainfall over the monsoon period from 1 June to 18 August 2020, all 14 districts in Kerala were
  • affected with 104 dead and 40 injured. Five districts in Kerala were flooded on 7 August 2020.
  • DECLARING THE DECREE: God through Jesus Christ by the power of the Holy Spirit has given a sign of the revival prophesied to exalt His name because "salvation is found in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given to mankind by which we must be saved." Amen.

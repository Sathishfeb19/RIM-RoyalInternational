Jithu C.S received salvation and a call to follow Jesus Christ of Nazareth (2014-2015) when he was a film school student (2013-2014) in Kerala. Jithu C.S received a graduation from film school on 31 December 2014 from the first Malayali Oscar winner, and on 1 January 2015, gave up everything to follow Jesus because the call of God must be honored by surrender.

God graciously supplied great faith to Jithu C.S to bless Filmstars through prophetic prayer, as the word of God teaches on three matters: love, prayer, and honor, concerning a Christian's responsibility towards people in this world. This is a journey of faith, it started when Jithu C.S prayed for Hrithik Roshan and Sussane Khan. A synopsis of this faith journey will be written in His time regarding to 6 Filmstars, which is a sure blessing to remind all that “God so love the people of this world, but He is not a respecter of persons.” Who will be the seventh? Rather, who should be the seventh?

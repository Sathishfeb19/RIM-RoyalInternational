DISTRICT PROPHECIES

DISTRICT: THIRUVANANTHAPURAM, KERALA, INDIA

  • DREAM: Jithu C.S saw a strike infront of the Secretariat, Trivandrum, Kerala. The Kerala Police brutally beat the protesters violently and some of them were bleeding blood. After the dream, as Jithu C.S was walking down the stairs, his mom read in the newspaper about the ongoing strike in infront of the Secretariat. Jithu C.S perceived in his spirit about the urgency of the call to pray for the world.
  • DATE OF THE DREAM: Unknown, since not record, must be in February 2021. Jithu C.S was unaware of the event.
  • DATE OF THE EVENT: 34 days, until 28 February 2021, as per the news.
  • DATE OF PUBLISHING THE DREAM: 27 February 2021, ROYAL CHURCH, the 1st blog of Jithu C.S (7 March 2020 - 17 January 2023).
  • DATE OF FULFILLMENT: 28 February 2021.
  • DECLARING THE DECREE: God through Jesus Christ by the power of the Holy Spirit performed a MIRACLE WITHIN 24 HOURS to exalt His name because, "salvation is found in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given to mankind by which we must be saved." Amen

DISTRICT: THIRUVANANTHAPURAM, KERALA, INDIA

  • KNOWLEDGE: Jithu C.S received a message of knowledge to pray against rainfall in Kerala, to stop flood, in the afternoon but falls into deep sleep. Unaware of the heavy rainfall, thunder and lightning in Trivandrum particularly, Jithu C.S woke up to witness the heavy rainfall, hence commanded the rain to stop by raising his hand, within minutes the rain stopped. And there a great calm.
  • DATE: 4 April 2022
  • DATE OF THE EVENT: 4 April 2022
  • DATE OF THE FULFILLMENT: 4 April 2022
  • DECLARING THE DECREE: God through Jesus Christ by the power of the Holy Spirit performed a MIRACLE WITHIN MINUTES to exalt His name because, "salvation is found in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given to men by which we must be saved." Amen
